## Enter the Dragon {#enter-the-dragon}

-   good in the action scenes, but otherwise slow. felt like in its
    attempts to hit the mainstream/prestige of its day, it cut out a lot
    of the fun of the genre. the characters cant be one note, so theyre
    harder to read at a glance. there must be motivation, so the amount
    of time given to the action---the real fun part---gets cut out. when
    the action does come though---and so may of these scenes obvious
    classics, down to their repetition in other places---it's capital g great
